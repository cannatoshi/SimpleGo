## Third-Party Software

### SimpleX Protocol (AGPL-3.0)

SimpleGo implements the SimpleX Messaging Protocol. The protocol specification is from:
- https://github.com/simplex-chat/simplexmq

### wolfSSL (GPLv2)

X448/Curve448 cryptographic operations.
- https://github.com/wolfSSL/wolfssl

### libsodium (ISC License)

Ed25519 and X25519 operations.
- https://github.com/jedisct1/libsodium

### mbedTLS (Apache 2.0)

TLS 1.3 and AES-GCM encryption.
- https://github.com/Mbed-TLS/mbedtls

### ESP-IDF (Apache 2.0)

Espressif IoT Development Framework.
- https://github.com/espressif/esp-idf
